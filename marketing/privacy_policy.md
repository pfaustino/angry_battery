# Privacy Policy for Angry Battery
**Effective Date:** January 2026

## 1. Introduction
Angry Battery ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
To provide battery-saving features, we may collect the following data:

*   **Usage Data:** To identify "power-hungry" apps, we collect data on which apps are running and their duration of use.
*   **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, and battery health statistics (temperature, voltage, capacity).
*   **Technical Identifiers:** We do not collect your name or email unless you contact support directly.

## 3. Permissions & Disclosures
Angry Battery requires the following sensitive permissions:

*   **Usage Access:** Used solely to identify background apps consuming battery. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
*   **Battery Optimization:** Required to allow the app to monitor health in the background.

## 4. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data exclusively to:
*   Analyze and display battery consumption.
*   Provide personalized tips to extend battery life.
*   Improve app performance and fix bugs.

## 5. Data Sharing & Third Parties
We do not sell your personal data.

We may use third-party analytics (like Firebase) to monitor app crashes. These services handle data according to their own privacy policies.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA 2026)
Under the latest 2026 regulations, you have the right to:

*   **Access/Delete:** Request the deletion of any data we have collected.
*   **Opt-Out:** Stop all data collection by uninstalling the app or revoking permissions in Settings.
*   **Limit Use:** You can limit the use of sensitive "Usage Access" at any time through your Android System Settings.
